Spend compute before spending follow-up time.

Extra repeats are a small computational cost compared with months spent chasing split-dependent candidate genes.

Three things to trust.

No grand biomarker claim. Just leakage control, repeated competition and external checks.

01

Keep selection inside the fold.

Every ranking step happens after the split. The held-out samples do not vote for the genes.

02

Stability can improve without buying accuracy.

Small-batch or consensus Top100 lists are more reproducible while internal CV5 performance is retained.

03

Baselines keep the claim honest.

XGBoost, regression and elastic net all see task signal; METABRIC transfer is used as a constraint, not a victory lap.
