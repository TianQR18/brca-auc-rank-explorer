BRCA Top2000 Gene Explorer

This interactive view starts from TCGA-BRCA RNA expression with more than 20,000 measured genes. Repeated small-batch random-subspace XGBoost gives genes many local opportunities to compete, then each task keeps a 2,000-gene candidate panel for a 100-rerun stability audit. The goal is fast biological triage: which genes repeatedly rank high, what they do, and how stable their RNA-level signal is in this task.
