BRCA Full-Rank AUC Explorer

This page audits candidate-gene ranking stability across 100 random train/test reruns. Every rerun assigns a full rank from 1 to 2000 to every gene; zero-gain genes are assigned rank 2000.

What this does

Each task uses a fixed Top2000 gene universe selected from the most interpretable small-batch panel, then reruns XGBoost under 100 random stratified splits.

AUC ranking

Per rerun score is 2000 * (2000 - rank) / 1999. A gene always ranked 2000 has AUC score 0. The leaderboard is sorted by mean AUC score.

Rank quartiles

Q1 is ranks 1-500, Q2 501-1000, Q3 1001-1500, and Q4 1501-2000. Zero-gain genes contribute to Q4.

Boundary

This is a stability audit of candidate-gene ranking. It is not causal mechanism evidence and is not a clinical diagnostic result.
